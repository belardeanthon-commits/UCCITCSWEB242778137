About Santiago, Ilocos Sur

Santiago, Ilocos Sur is a charming town rich in culture, nature, and modern attractions. Visitors can explore scenic accommodations like Vitalis Villas, admire architectural creativity at Aluminak, and enjoy natural wonders like Mapisi Rock.

The town is also known for local crafts, cuisine, and friendly communities, making it a perfect destination for tourists seeking both relaxation and adventure.

Supporting local tourism in Santiago ensures that its natural and cultural treasures are preserved for future generations to experience.
